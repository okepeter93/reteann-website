Pillar Three · Economics
Unit Economics

From opaque spend to measurable value.

AI without accountability is a cost centre. Reteann's Unit Economics model gives your organisation complete cost-per-outcome visibility — tracking what every AI interaction costs against the revenue and output it generates, across every role and workflow.

1

Capture AI Usage

Track who is using AI, in which role, for which task — with full telemetry across tools and platforms.

2

Map to Workflows

Allocate usage to specific workflows — Sales, Ops, Marketing, Finance — so costs are attributed correctly.

3

Assign Value & Revenue

Connect each workflow to the business output it generates — leads, contracts, reports, customer interactions.

4

Analyse ROI & Margin

Calculate cost per outcome, ROI per workflow, and net margin — automated, not manual.

5

Optimise — Scale or Cut

Clear decisions: what to invest in more, what to reduce, and where AI is genuinely creating value.
